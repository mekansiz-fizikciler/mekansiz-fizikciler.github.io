Feza Gürsey: Celebrating an exemplary life

To commemorate 100th age of Feza Gürsey who was one of the most extra-ordinary physicists from Turkey, we are honored to have Prof. Dr. Kameshwar Wali from Syracuse University, who was an old collegue and friend of Feza Gürsey and also one of the best renowned theoretical physicists alive. In this special seminar, Prof. Wali will tell us about Feza Gursey. The event will be held via Zoom, and simulatenously broadcasted in our YouTube channel.
